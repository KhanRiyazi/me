IT Career Roadmap

1

Foundation & Basics

Start with fundamental IT concepts, basic programming (Python recommended), and computer networking principles. Complete free introductory courses from official platforms.

2

Specialization Choice

Choose your path: Cloud Computing, Web Development, Cybersecurity, Data Science, or DevOps. Focus on official certifications from recognized industry leaders.

3

Hands-on Practice

Build projects, use free tiers of cloud services, contribute to open source, and create a portfolio. Practical experience is crucial for landing your first role.

4

Certification & Job Search

Earn industry-recognized certifications, optimize your resume/LinkedIn, network with professionals, and apply for entry-level positions or internships.

5

Career Advancement

Continue learning advanced topics, earn senior-level certifications, specialize further, and consider freelance/consulting opportunities for increased earnings.
